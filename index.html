The Artist

Before you knew the name, you heard the music.

Hersey is a New York DJ and producer channeling the raw energy of early-2000s tech house with modern dub touches, driving funk-inspired bass lines, and inventive sampling.

"In My Life" has been road-tested on the world's best dancefloors — Circoloco DC10, FUSE, Sonar, Hï Ibiza, Warehouse Project. His debut EP on Berg Audio (December 2025), with a remix from Anil Aras, earned support from the industry's most respected selectors, and the lead single charted in Beatport's Top 100. In 2026: the vinyl debut of "In My Life" on Elements Selected Pieces Vol. 4, alongside Traumer, Kolter, and Josh Butler.

The Machine

Continuum

Software that listens to live music, reads its emotional and thematic shape in real time, and turns it into generative visuals and lighting.

The visual at the top of this page is the idea in miniature: a signal goes in, a feeling comes out, and the feeling drives the light. At full scale, Continuum does this for live venues — a real-time emotion model listening alongside the crowd.

Built with AI as the collaborator. The codebase written with Claude Code. The research — music-emotion recognition, model architectures — run with agents. A larger model distilled into a small local one fast enough to keep up live on stage. Agents handle the work; I focus on taste and direction.
